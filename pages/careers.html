Working at AETERNA

Build Your Career in Technical Excellence

AETERNA Inspection & Technical Services LLC keeps industry running. From power generation and HVAC to third-party inspection and NDT, we deliver solutions that never pause. Across the Middle East, our clients in oil & gas, petrochemical, power, and construction rely on our expertise and the latest technologies to keep their operations running without interruption.

We are practical problem solvers. We prioritise safety, bring passion and pace, and put our clients' interests first. If you bring energy and expertise, we will give you the platform to grow.

Our Culture

How We Work

Our culture is built on practical problem-solving, technical excellence, and an uncompromising commitment to safety.

Safety First, Always

Nothing is more important than our people going home safe every day. We invest heavily in our safety culture so everyone understands their role in keeping themselves and others safe. Every team member has the authority to stop unsafe work.

Practical Problem Solvers

We own every challenge. Our instinct is to make our clients' and colleagues' lives smarter, simpler, and easier. We do not over-complicate — we find solutions and deliver them at pace.

Inclusion & Integrity

We hire from diverse backgrounds because diverse experience makes us stronger. We conduct business with transparency, honesty, and ethical rigour. Every voice is heard and respected.

Passion & Pace

We are entrepreneurial, agile, and low on ego. We bring a positive, can-do attitude and turn thinking into action quickly. Our clients trust us to accelerate their business.

Innovation & Sustainability

Technology That Leads
